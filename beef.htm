 Fillet with Mustard Sauce

  • 4 4-oz fillets Mignon
  • 3 Tbsp pepper, coarsely ground
  • 4 shallots, peeled and minced
  • 1/2 cup tawny port wine
  • 2 Tbsp spicy mustard
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • salt and pepper

Heat oven to 350°F. Coat steaks with pepper on both sides. Sear steaks in very hot cast-iron skillet for 5 min on each side. Place steaks on oven-safe dish and finish cooking in oven for 8 min (medium-rare). Let steaks rest while you make sauce.
Over low heat, cook the shallots in the same cast-iron skillet you used before. When tender, add port wine, and cook for a couple of minutes while stirring. Add mustard and cream. Bring to simmer and season with salt and pepper. Place steaks on serving plates and spoon sauce over them. Serve with baked potato and salad. (4 servings)

 Picadillo

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1 tsp cumin, ground
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tomato, coarsely chopped
  • 1/4 cup almonds, slivered
  • 1/4 cup green (pimento-stuffed) olives, sliced

Brown meat in skillet. Drain fat. Add the next 8 ingredients, cover, and simmer on low heat for 15 min. Add tomato and 1/4 cup water, cover, and simmer for an additional 30 min. In a dry skillet toast almonds until golden. Slice olives. Stir in almonds and olives before serving. Serve with corn chips, warm flour tortillas, and salad. (4 servings)

 Perfect Stroganoff

  • 1 lb fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 lb beef tenderloin, cut in pieces
  • 2 cups beef bouillon
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 2 cups non-fat sour cream

Cook mushroom, onion, and butter in a large deep skillet. Remove mushrooms and onion, and in the same skillet, brown the beef. Save 3/4 cup bouillon; add the remainder together with ketchup, garlic, and salt. Cover and simmer for 15 min. Mix saved bouillon with flour and add the mixture plus mushroom and onion to the meat. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Stir in sour cream and heat until hot. Serve over wide egg noodles. (8 servings)

Charlotte's mother's basic chili recipe that, as most, tastes better the second day. We call it . . .
 Lucille's Chili

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can tomatoes with chilies
  • 1 can whole tomatoes
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 8-oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 can chili beans
  • 3 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Brown meat and drain fat. Add onion, pepper, and sauté until soft. Add other ingredients plus 1/2 cup of water and simmer for 45 min. Serve with salty crackers, chopped raw onion, and shredded cheddar cheese. (4 servings)

 Beef Patties á la Lindström

  • 1 lb ground beef (90% lean)
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 4 Tbsp onion, finely chopped
  • 3 Tbsp capers, drained and finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup pickled beets, drained and finely chopped
  • liquid from pickled beets (if needed)
  • butter for browning

Mix beef, egg yolks, cream, and spices. Gently fold in the onion, capers, and beets. If too thick, add some (1/4 cup) beet liquid. Shape into round patties, 2" in diameter patties and brown in butter about 4 minutes per side in a skillet. Transfer patties to a heavy pot as they are done. When you are finished browning add 1/2 cup water to the skillet and let it come to a boil. Transfer the liquid to the to the pot and simmer under cover for 20 min. Serve with mashed potatoes and whole-berry cranberry sauce. (4 servings)

 Low-Carb Chili

  • 1 lb ground beef (90-93% lean)
  • 8 green onions, sliced
  • 1 8-oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 15-oz can black soy beans
  • 2 10-oz cans Rotel Diced Tomatoes w/ Chilies
  • 2 tsp coriander, ground
  • 2 tsp cumin, ground
  • 1 tsp oregano, dried
  • 1 tsp allspice, ground
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Brown beef in a little oil. Add all the other ingredients plus a 1/4 cup of water and simmer for 30 min. Serve with corn bread, cracker, and shredded cheddar cheese. (4-5 servings)

 Texas Chili

  • 2 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup chili powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 8-oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 14.5-oz can beef broth
  • 1 14.5-oz can chicken broth
  • 2 1/2 tsp cumin, ground
  • 2 tsp garlic, granulated
  • 2 tsp onion, granulated
  • 1 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper, ground
  • 1/2 tsp oregano, dried
  • 1/2 tsp allspice, ground

In deep pan/skillet, brown meat and onion. Drain all the liquid. Add chili powder, salt, tomato sauce, and broths. Cover and simmer for 30 min. Add the remaining spices and simmer for an additional 30 min. Serve with corn bread, cracker, sour cream, chopped onion, and shredded cheddar cheese. (4 servings)

 Old Settlers Baked Beans

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 16-oz can pork and beans
  • 1 16-oz can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 16-oz can lima beans, drained
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup barbeque sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp prepared mustard

Heat oven to 350°F. Brown beef, bacon, and onion. Turn off the heat and drain. Mix in the rest of ingredients and add to the meat and onion mixture. Mix well and pour into sprayed 9"x13" pan. Bake for 45 min. (6 servings)

 Swedish Meatballs

  • 2/3 lb ground beef (93% lean)
  • 1/3 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 red onion, graded
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp allspice, ground
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 hot dog bun, cut into small cubes and soaked in 2 oz hot water
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 1 1/2 cups beef stock
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • salt & pepper

Heat oven to 400°F. Mix salt, allspice, egg yolk, and wet bread. Blend (with your hands) in the meat and onion until the mixture is smooth and uniform. Shape into 1-inch balls and place on greased baking sheet. Bake for 20 min, tuning the meatballs once. Turn on the broiler for 6 min, turning the meatballs once. While the meatballs are baking, melt butter in skillet. Add flour and mix until smooth. Add beef stock a little at a time, mixing and keeping it smooth. Add Worcestershire sauce and let it come to a simmer. Add cream, meatballs, and spices and allow it to simmer on low heat for 10 min. Serve with lingonberry preserves or whole-berry cranberry sauce. (4 servings)
