 Vegetarian Lasagna

  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 6-oz jar mushrooms, cut
  • 1 can tomatoes, Italian stewed
  • 10 green olives, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp basil
  • 20 oz spinach, frozen whole, thawed
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg, ground
  • 12 oz Mozzarella cheese, low-fat
  • 8 oz cottage cheese, low-fat
  • 1/4 cup milk

Boil noodles according to directions. Brown onions, garlic, and mushrooms in oil. Add tomatoes, olives, and basil; simmer 5 min. Mix nutmeg, cottage cheese, and 3/4 of Mozzarella cheese in a bowl. In 9x13-inch pan, spread a thin layer (3-4 Tbsp) red sauce. Now create layers with 3 noodles, 1/3 of spinach, 1/3 of red sauce, and 1/3 of the cheese mixture. Repeat with the rest of the noodles. Top off with remaining cheese and pour the milk around the edge of pan. Cover with foil and bake 30 min at 425°F. Uncover (save foil) and bake an additional 5 min or until lightly brown. Remove from oven, cover with foil and wait 15 min before serving with a side salad. Chianti, what else? (6 servings)

 Cincinnati Chili

  • 8 oz penne pasta
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 8-oz cans tomato sauce
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin, ground
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon, ground
  • 1/8 tsp allspice, ground
  • 1/8 tsp cloves, ground
  • 1/8 tsp Cayenne pepper, ground
  • 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1 15-oz can red kidney beans, drained
  • 1/4 cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded

Cook pasta according to directions. Meanwhile, saute beef, onion, pepper, and garlic until done. Add the sauces, all the remaining spices, and 1/4 cup of water. Bring to a boil and reduce to simmer. Heat the kidney beans in the microwave. To serve, use deep bowls and top pasta with chili, beans, and cheese. Serve with your favorite beer. (4 servings)

 Yellow Pea Soup

  • 18 oz yellow peas; dry, whole, with hulls
  • 10 cups of water
  • 1 yellow onion, coarsly cut
  • 12 oz country cured ham
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp marjoram
  • 1 tsp thyme

Soak peas in water for 24 h. Bring to a boil with onion, ham, and salt. Simmer for 1 h, and then remove the ham. Trim off excess fat, and cut ham in small pieces. Return ham, and add marjoram and thyme. Heat to a boil. Serve with Swedish Punsch and crapes. (6-8 serving.)

 Chile-roasted Sweet Potato

  • 3 medium sweet potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp course salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper, ground

Preheat oven to 425°F. Cut each potato lengthwise into eight wedges. In bowl, toss potato wedges with the remaining ingredients. Arrange on baking sheet lined with parchment paper, cut side down. Roast potatoes until tender, 20+ min. (4 servings)

 Creole Onion Soup

  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced leeks, white parts
  • 2 cups red onions, sliced
  • 3 cups Vidalia onions, sliced
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper, ground
  • 1/2 tsp mustard, dried
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp basil
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 2 Tbsp garlic, minced
  • 4 Tbsp dry roux
  • 2 quarts beef broth, heated
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • toasted French bread and shredded Swiss cheese

In large covered pot over medium heat, cook leeks and onions in the butter and oil for 10 min. Add salt and sugar and cook uncovered, stirring often, for 20 min. Add the dry spices and cook for 10 more min, stirring often. Add garlic plus dry roux and stir for 3 min. Slowly add beef broth, while stirring to keep smooth consistency. Add the wine, reduce the heat and simmer for 40 min. Remove from heat. In over-safe serving bowls, portion out soup, placing a slice of bread and shredded cheese on top. Place under oven broiler until brown. (8 servings)

 Fresh Tomato Salsa

  • 1-1.5 lbs fresh tomatoes, seeds removed and chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 1 jalapeno chile pepper, cleaned and finely diced

 Spinach and Cheese Strata

  • 10 oz frozen spinach
  • 1 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt, divided
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper, divided
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg, ground
  • 8 cups sourdough bread, cubed (3/4"), divided
  • 6 oz coarsely graded Gruyere cheese, divided
  • 2 oz finly graded Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, divided
  • 2 3/4 cups milk
  • 9 eggs
  • 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard

Squeeze the thawed spinach to remove as much liquid as possible, then chop. Cook onion in butter in skillet until tender. Add 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper, nutmeg, and spinach. Stir and remove from heat. To a buttered 9x13" pan, add 1/3 of bread. Cover with 1/3 of the onion mixture, followed by 1/3 of the two cheeses. Repeat in two more layers. Wisk eggs and blend in the rest of the ingredients (milk, mustard, salt, pepper). Distribute the egg/milk mixture evenly over the pan of bread. Cover and chill in refrigerator over night. Remove pan from refrigerator 45 min before baking without the cover in preheated 350°F oven for 45-55 min until golden brown. Let stand for 10 min before serving. (6 servings)

Spinach and Apple Dip

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 16 oz sour cream
  • 1 pkg Knorr Vegetable Recipe Mix
  • 10 oz frozen spinach, thawed, and squeezed to remove liquid
  • 1 1/2 Granny Smith apples, pealed, cored, and chopped in small cube
  • 1 8-oz can water chestnuts, drained and chopped (optional)
  • 3 green onions, chopped (optional)

Mix mayonaise and sour cream well. Add in the vegerable mix and spinach. Blend well. Fold in apple pieces. Let it rest in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or over night. (20 servings)

Green Bean Casserole

  • 2 10.5 oz cans of (Cambell's) Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (subsitute with Condensed Cream of Broccoli or other)
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 14.5 oz cans of Del Monte Cut Green Beans, drained
  • 6 oz French's Original Crispy Fried Onions, divided

Heat oven to 350°F. In 9x13-inch pan, mix the first four ingredients until uniform. Blend in the beans and half of the fried onions. Bake in oven for 30 min or until bubbly. Sprinkle the remaining fried onions on top and bake for another 8 min. Remove from oven and let it rest for 15 min before serving (12 servings)
